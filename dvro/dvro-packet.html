DVRO Packet Checklist

This is the “bridge” from understanding the DVRO process to producing a printable, organized packet.

Checklist
  1. Incident story: a clean summary + a timeline (what happened, when, where, who witnessed it).
  2. Requested protections: exactly what you want the court to order (and why each is necessary).
  3. Exhibits: screenshots, emails, police reports, medical notes, photos—each labeled and ordered.
  4. Service plan: identify who will serve and how proof of service will be filed.
  5. Print set: one clean “court copy” + one “service copy” + one “your copy”.
