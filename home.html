About SharpeSystem

SharpeSystem is a structured decision framework for navigating complex systems. Legal processes are currently the most developed branch. Additional branches — including healthcare, immigration, education, and licensing pathways — are being built.

  • Request for Order (RFO) — structured preparation and drafting workflow
  • Triage — classify what you’re dealing with before acting
  • Decision Trees — guided pathways for defined procedural systems
