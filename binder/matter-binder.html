Matter Binder

A neutral container for timelines, documents, and decisions — without advice. This is a conceptual walkthrough (no account, no storage, no portal access yet).

What this does

  • Centralizes facts, dates, and documents in one place
  • Preserves timelines before details are lost
  • Creates a clean record you can share later
  • Reduces emotional drift by forcing structure

What this does not do

  • Does not provide legal advice
  • Does not recommend strategy
  • Does not replace counsel
Design principle: clarity over urgency. records over memory.
