This is a proof-of-concept overview of SharpeSystem as procedural orientation and decision support. It is not legal advice, not legal strategy, and not a substitute for counsel.

What you’re being invited to review

SharpeSystem is a neutral orientation layer: decision trees, scope notices, and process prompts intended to reduce procedural drift and improve the quality of client-provided information.

Why this may matter in intake

  • Cleaner intake: fewer scattered narratives, more structured facts.
  • Lower friction: clients stop re-litigating basics and start answering the right questions.
  • Better documentation: consistent “what happened / what’s next” records.
  • Boundary clarity: explicit non-advice framing throughout.
Positioning: this is not asking you to adopt anything. It’s a short preview plus optional feedback.
