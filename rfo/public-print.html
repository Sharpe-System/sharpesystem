Orange County filing guide (preview)

This is a placeholder preview. The paid module will include step-by-step filing + service checklists.

  1. Confirm your requested orders are specific and enforceable.
  2. Assemble your declaration + exhibit index.
  3. Print required copies (varies by filing method).
  4. File with the court and obtain a hearing date.
  5. Serve the other party per rules and file proof of service.
  6. Prepare a hearing binder and a short oral outline.
