Attorney-facing Decision support • Procedural orientation • Not legal advice

For Attorneys

SharpeSystem is an orientation layer for people who are overwhelmed by procedure. It reduces noise, improves client clarity, and standardizes intake artifacts—without drafting filings or giving advice.

What it is

  • Structured procedural orientation (decision trees, timelines, terminology)
  • Client calibration: “what matters next” vs. emotional spirals
  • Clean artifact capture: dates, orders, pleadings, communications, exhibits
  • Consistency: repeatable flows across similar case types

What it is not

  • Not legal advice
  • Not a substitute for counsel
  • Not a filing generator (at least initially)
  • Not a “strategy” tool for escalation

Why attorneys might care

  • Fewer resets: less time spent re-explaining procedure across meetings
  • Cleaner intake: clients arrive with structured facts, dates, and documents
  • Lower friction: clients understand constraints and dead ends earlier
  • Better scope control: keeps work inside defined tasks and timelines

The system is designed to be calm, factual, and non-reassuring—reducing performative conflict and improving signal.

Subject: Orientation before our next meeting

Before we meet, please review this orientation resource. It is decision support / procedural orientation only (not legal advice). The goal is to reduce confusion and help you organize the key facts, dates, and documents so we can use our time efficiently.

Start here: /home.html
If conflict is escalating, review: /high-conflict-risk-awareness.html
