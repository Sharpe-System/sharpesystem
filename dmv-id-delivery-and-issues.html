Delivery, Problems, and Next Moves

After the DMV visit, the ID card is produced and mailed. If something goes wrong, the goal is not perfectionâ€”it is steady progress: fix the issue, document the steps taken, and keep the chain moving.

Common follow-up actions
  • Confirm the mailing address on record is correct
  • Track expected delivery time and keep the receipt
  • Respond to document verification requests
  • Report non-delivery or returned mail promptly
  • Request correction if the name, date of birth, or address is wrong
  • Request a replacement if the card is lost or stolen

Completion means the card has been received, or a clear follow-up step has been started with documentation saved.
