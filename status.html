Status

Scope, boundaries, and current state of the SharpeSystem project.

What this is

  • A decision-support framework for procedural orientation in high-stakes situations
  • Designed to help users identify where they are, what matters next, and what deadlines or risks may exist
  • Built to reduce confusion, false urgency, and avoidable procedural mistakes

What this is not

  • Not legal advice
  • Not legal strategy
  • Not a substitute for an attorney or other qualified professional
  • Not a tool for confrontation, leverage, or escalation
Important: Content is intentionally general and non-directive. Jurisdiction-specific rules, tactics, and filings are outside the scope of this project.

Current build state

  • Core site structure and navigation are live
  • Decision tree framework is in active development
  • Risk Awareness and Amicable paths are operational
  • Additional decision branches will be added iteratively
  • Custom domain will be connected after registrar transfer window
