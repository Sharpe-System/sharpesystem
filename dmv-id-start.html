A California identification (ID) card is issued by the Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) and serves as official proof of identity and California residency. It does not grant driving privileges.

This flow organizes the basic requirements and steps commonly used when applying for or replacing a California ID card.

Common ID card purposes
  • Identity verification for services or benefits
  • Proof of age or name
  • Housing or employment documentation
  • School or medical registration
