I’m not sure what to do

This page is for the moment where you feel behind, unsure what matters, and you need help even naming what you’re dealing with.

Step 1 — What kind of situation is this?

  • Family/custody/divorce (relationships, kids, money, court orders)
  • Protective order / restraining order (no-contact orders, hearings, alleged abuse)
  • Criminal case (arraignment, plea, probation, court dates)
  • Not sure — keep reading

Step 2 — Is there a deadline or a hearing?

If you have a court date or a filing deadline, that’s usually the first anchor.

  • I have a hearing within 14 days
  • I have a deadline within 30 days
  • No known deadline — I’m trying to stabilize and plan

Step 3 — What outcome are you trying to create?

  • Protect my time with my child / custody / visitation structure
  • Reduce conflict and communicate cleanly
  • Respond to a filing / avoid default / avoid procedural mistakes
  • Understand what stage I’m in and what “next” actually means
